Meet the Founders

Aubrey Norris

Founder/CEO

Born and raised in Staten Island NY where he attended Port Richmond HS. There, he became a QB sensation; leading the PSAL in pass completion, passing yards and touchdowns during his 03' 04' season. Some of his major career highlights are Two time All City All Star, Two Time Daily News All Star, Two Time Staten Island Advance All Star and Daily News player of the year 04.

During his undergrad career, he was the Quarterback for Hargrave Miltary Academy in 04'; one of the nations top Prep School. From there, he went on to QB for Central Connecticut State University. During his freshman season he was named NEC Conference Rookie of the year in 05'. He lead the Blue Devils to two Conference Championships in 05' and 09. Aubrey left a lagacy there for having the Highest QB Rating in CCSU school history. Later on in his career, he recieved the NEC All conference honors 09. His experience even extends to Arena football as the Quarterback for the Florida Tarpons (AIFL) and then Lehigh Valley SteelHawks (IFL)
